Research Interests

Random matrices, Integrable Systems, Analysis of integrable dispersive PDEs. Riemann-Hilbert problems. Deep Learning theory, Optimization.

My research is focused on the use of Riemann-Hilbert techniques to study physically meaningful quantities arising in the field of Random Matrix Theory or Integrable PDEs, in particular their asymptotic behaviour in certain limit or critical regime and their connection with integrable systems (Lax pair and Painlevé equations).

I also have a keen interest in applications of Random Matrix Theory and Integrable Systems to Generalization analysis and Optimization in Machine Learning.

For the years 2022-2023, my research was supported by the Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC) - Discovery Grant.
