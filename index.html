I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Mathematics & Computing Science at Saint Mary's University (Halifax, NS).
I am also Affiliate Assistant Professor at the Mathematics & Statistics Department at Concordia University and Associate Member / Collaborator at Mila Institute, both in Montréal, QC.

This summer (2022) I'll be a Simons Fellow at the Isaac Newton Institute at the University of Cambridge, UK.
I am co-PI of the CIFAR AI Catalyst Grant “Rethinking generalization and model diagnostics in modern machine learning” with I. Mitliagkas (Mila Institute, U. de Montréal) and M. Erdogdu (Vector Instute, U. of Toronto).
